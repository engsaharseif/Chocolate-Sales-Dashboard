# Chocolate-Sales-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built to analyze chocolate product performance across multiple countries and time periods.  The dashboard provides key business insights into sales trends, product performance, and regional contributions, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.
## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total sales and growth trends
- Identify top-performing countries and products
- Track monthly sales performance
- Evaluate sales team effectiveness

---

## 📌 Key Features
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Boxes, Growth %
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Country Comparison
- MoM Growth Analysis
- Salesperson Performance Tracking
- Top 7 Best-Selling Products

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- June recorded the highest sales, while February had the lowest performance.
- Australia is the top-performing market.
- Some countries show growth potential and need improvement.
- A small group of products drives the majority of revenue.
